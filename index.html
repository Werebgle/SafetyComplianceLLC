Welcome to Safety Compliance, LLC

Safety Compliance, LLC is a family owned business operated to assist manufacturing, construction, medical and other industries with emergency response, business continuity planning, as well as occupational safety and health compliance. We have an unmatched level of expertise with over 50 years of combined experience in fire protection and OSHA training and compliance in both the public and private sectors.

For any business, the health and safety of its employees should be paramount. This emphasis on employee safety and health should originate from the notion that all employees deserve to work in an environment conducive to being injury free. Many businesses have been negatively impacted over the past few years. Budget cuts often include downsizing of full-time, on-site safety professionals which is not in the best interest of the company nor its employees. Safety Compliance, LLC can help a company ensure the safety and health of all employees.

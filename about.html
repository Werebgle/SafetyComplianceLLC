About Us

Who We Are

Safety Compliance, LLC is a family owned business operated to assist manufacturing, construction, medical and other industries with emergency response, business continuity planning, as well as occupational safety and health compliance. We have an unmatched level of expertise with over 50 years of combined experience in fire protection and OSHA training and compliance in both the public and private sectors.

Mission Statement

Provide our clients with the highest quality and knowledgeable safety consultation while maintaining 100% customer satisfaction.

Business Philosophy

  • 100% customer service and satisfaction
  • Provide complete and total employee health and safety for our clients
  • Provide clients with ALL OSHA compliance, safety and training needs
  • Provide planning and prevention guidelines
